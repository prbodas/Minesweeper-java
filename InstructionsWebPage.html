How to Play 

~Overview~

Minesweeper is a game of luck and skill. The board contains several cells. "Safe" cells reveal a number when clicked, "dangerous" cells explode. The objective of the game is to reveal all of the safe cells.

~Gameplay~

Gameplay begins with safe cells, which will either reveal a number or reveal the numbers of the closest numbered cells around it. For example, one safe cell might reveal a number two. A blank safe cell would reveal the numbers of the closest numbered cells surrounding it. 

The numbers you will uncover reveal how many mines are hidden in the eight surrounding squares. These numbers can be used, alone and in tandem, to guess which cells have mines.  You will lose the game if you click on a mine. To win, you must mark all of the cells concealing mines with a flag by clicking the right mouse button. A second right-click will display a question mark on top of the hidden cell, and a third will revert the cell back to its original state. To reveal a cell, left-click. If you reveal a mine, you lose the game.
